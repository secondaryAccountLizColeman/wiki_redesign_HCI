Galaxy in the constellation Ursa Major

Characteristics
TypeIm/BCD C[1]
Other designations
NGC 4144, IRAS F12074+4644, 2MASX J12095860+4627258, UGC 7151, PGC 38688[1]

NGC 4144 is a barred spiral galaxy in the constellation Ursa Major, and it was discovered by William Herschel on April 10, 1788.[2]
